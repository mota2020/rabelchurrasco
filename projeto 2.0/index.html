About Us

Rebel American BBQ is not just a restaurant, it's a tradition. Founded by Chef Flavio, our establishment has become a cornerstone of the barbecue community. With years of experience and a deep passion for barbecue, Chef Flavio brings the authentic flavors of American BBQ to every dish.

Our mission is to provide an unparalleled dining experience, combining high-quality ingredients with exceptional service. Whether you're a barbecue enthusiast or a first-time visitor, we promise a meal that will tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving more.
